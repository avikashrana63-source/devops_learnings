1. What is Disk & Storage Management?
It means:
Manage disks (HDD/SSD)
Create partitions
Mount storage
Expand storage (LVM)

Why it is used?
Add new disk to server
Store application data
Manage logs & backups
Increase storage without downtime

###########################################################################################
Mounting (mount, umount)
What is Mounting?
Mounting = attach disk to a folder (directory)
Linux me disk direct access nahi hota
→ usko kisi folder me mount karna padta hai

Commands:
1. Check disks (lsblk)
2. Mount disk (mount /dev/sdb1 /mnt)
3. Unmount disk (umount /mnt)

###########################################################################################
Partitions (fdisk)
What is Partition?
Partition = divide disk into parts
Example:
/dev/sda1
/dev/sda2

commands: 
1. Open fdisk (fdisk /dev/sdb)
